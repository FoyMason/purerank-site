About PureRank Strategies

We're a digital marketing agency that believes every business deserves marketing that actually works.

The Founder

Mason Foy

Founder & CEO, PureRank Strategies

Mason Foy founded PureRank Strategies with a simple belief: every business deserves marketing that actually works. After years of watching businesses waste money on agencies that delivered flashy reports but no real results, he knew there had to be a better way.

Based in St. George, Utah, Mason combines data-driven strategy with hands-on execution. He doesn't hand your account off to a junior team member or rely on set-it-and-forget-it tools. Every strategy is custom-built, every campaign is actively managed, and every dollar is tracked back to real business outcomes.

PureRank Strategies was built on three principles: transparency (you always know exactly where your money goes), accountability (if it's not working, we fix it), and results (vanity metrics don't pay the bills). Today, PureRank serves businesses nationwide — from local service companies to e-commerce brands to SaaS startups.
